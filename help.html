Help & Support

Welcome to the Lead Bridge Help Center. Here are some common FAQs:

💡 How do I sign up?

Click on the “Signup” page and enter your name, email, and password.

💻 How do I access courses?

After logging in, click on any course card on the Home page to start learning.

🧾 How can I edit my profile?

Go to your Profile page and click “Edit Profile” to update your details.

Use the Contact page form or email us at support@leadbridge.com.
